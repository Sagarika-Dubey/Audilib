The Couple at No. 9 by Claire Douglas is a gripping domestic thriller that follows Sophie and Neil as they befriend their neighbors, Elsie and Ben, at their new home. As secrets unravel, Sophie suspects something sinister at No. 9, leading to a suspenseful and twist-filled narrative that keeps readers on the edge of their seats.

"The Good Lie" by Robin Brande is a contemporary young adult novel about Emily, who makes a pact with her friends to tell only the truth for a week. As she navigates the challenges of honesty, Emily discovers the power of authenticity and its impact on her relationships and self-discovery.

Imperfect Magic by Vicki L. Weavil is a captivating fantasy novel following Arlo, a young wizard on a perilous quest to retrieve a dangerous magical artifact. Filled with adventure, friendship, and self-discovery, it immerses readers in a vividly imagined world of mythical creatures and ancient secrets.

Set in England at the turn of the 20th century, Wallace's crime novel The Daffodil Mystery follows the mysterious circumstances under which shop owner Lyne has been murdered. Accordingly, it is up to detective Jack Tarling and his trusted Chinese assistant to solve the case and reach an appropriate and just resolution. Moreover, the happenings within the novel are intensified by the colorful set of characters, which are marked by their plausible façade.

"I Love When You Lie" by Kristen Bird is a thrilling domestic suspense novel that follows the intertwined lives of three women whose secrets and lies threaten to unravel their carefully constructed worlds. As their deceptions spiral out of control, the stakes rise higher, leading to a shocking and explosive climax. With its intricate plot and gripping storytelling, this book keeps readers guessing until the very end.

"The House Beyond the Dunes" by A. S. Peterson is a gripping mystery set in a secluded coastal town. When a young woman discovers an abandoned house, she becomes ensnared in its dark secrets and the enigmatic history of its former occupants. As she delves deeper, she realizes that some secrets are best left undisturbed.
